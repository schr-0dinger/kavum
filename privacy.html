Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Identity of the Developer

PrescoRx is developed and operated by Dr. Kailas Nath K M, a Registered Medical Practitioner (MBBS) in India and a solo software developer.

As both a physician and the developer, I have designed this app to support clinical workflows while maintaining the privacy and security standards required for medical documentation. This Privacy Policy describes how I collect, use, and share information when you use PrescoRx.

Account and Professional Information

I collect the details you provide to set up and use PrescoRx on Android, including your name, email address, medical registration number, qualification, specialization, clinic details, and phone number. If you subscribe to PrescoRx Pro, I also store your subscription status and expiry date to manage your access.

Prescription and Patient Data

PrescoRx stores the prescriptions you create, which may include patient information such as name, age, gender, diagnosis, medicines, and consultation notes. This data is treated as sensitive personal data and is encrypted end-to-end using AES-256-GCM on your device before being stored in the cloud. Your signature image and clinic logo are saved in app storage on your device and used to generate prescriptions.

How I Use Your Data

Your information is used to authenticate your account, complete your doctor profile, support your consultation documentation workflow, generate and manage prescriptions, verify and manage your Pro subscription, and operate core app features. I do not sell your personal data.

Google and Other Service Providers

PrescoRx relies on Firebase for authentication and encrypted prescription storage, Firebase Cloud Functions for subscription verification with Google Play, Google Sign-In for optional account access, Google Play Billing for subscriptions, and AdMob to show non-personalized ads in the free version of the app. These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

I do not sell or share personal or patient data with AdMob or any other third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Retention

Prescription and profile data are retained until you delete them or delete your account. Subscription status data is updated automatically when your subscription renews or expires. Deleting your account removes your doctor profile, prescriptions, and locally saved signature and logo files associated with the app.

Your Rights (DPDPA Compliance)

In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) of India, you have the right to:
• Access a summary of your personal data being processed.
• Request the correction or completion of inaccurate or incomplete data.
• Request the erasure of your data.
• Withdraw your consent at any time.

From the app you can sign out or permanently delete your account and all associated data. Android permission choices can be managed at any time from Settings › Apps › PrescoRx › Permissions. To exercise further rights, contact me at support.prescorx@gmail.com.

Security

Sensitive prescription and patient data are encrypted end-to-end on your device using AES-256-GCM before being stored in the cloud. Data is transmitted over HTTPS, and access controls ensure that each doctor can only access their own records.

Consent

By using PrescoRx, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. For the processing of sensitive personal and patient data, I will seek your explicit consent via a prominent in-app disclosure before any collection takes place.
