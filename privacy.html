Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Identity of the Developer

PrescoRx is developed and operated by Dr. Kailas Nath K M, a Registered Medical Practitioner (MBBS) in India and a solo software developer.

As both a physician and the developer, I have designed this app to support clinical workflows while maintaining the privacy and security standards required for medical documentation. This Privacy Policy describes how I collect, use, and share information when you use PrescoRx.

Account and Professional Information

I collect the details you provide to set up and use PrescoRx on Android, including your name, email address, medical registration number, qualification, specialization, clinic details, and phone number.

Prescription and Signature Data

I store the prescription records you create. If you capture or choose a signature image, that image is saved in app storage on your Android device and used to place your signature on generated prescriptions.

Patient Data

PrescoRx allows doctors to create and store prescriptions that may include patient information such as name, age, gender, diagnosis, and prescribed medications. This data is entered by the doctor and handled as sensitive personal data. It is stored securely using Firebase services and is not used for any purpose other than providing core app functionality.

How I Use Your Data

Your information is used to authenticate your account, complete your doctor profile, support your consultation documentation workflow, generate and manage prescriptions, and operate core app features. I do not sell your personal data.

Google and Other Service Providers

PrescoRx uses Firebase (by Google) for authentication and secure storage of prescription and profile data. Google Sign-In may be used for account access. Google Play Billing is used for in-app purchases. AdMob is used to display non-personalized advertisements. These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

I do not sell or share personal or patient data with AdMob or any other third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Retention

Prescription and profile data are retained until you delete them or delete your account. Deleting your account removes your doctor profile, prescriptions, and locally saved signature files associated with the app.

Your Rights (DPDPA Compliance)

In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) of India, you have the right to:
• Access a summary of your personal data being processed.
• Request the correction or completion of inaccurate or incomplete data.
• Request the erasure of your data.
• Withdraw your consent at any time.
To exercise these rights, please contact me via the support email listed below.

Security

I use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure backend infrastructure provided by Firebase. All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. Access controls ensure that each doctor can only access their own records.

Consent

By using PrescoRx, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. For the processing of sensitive personal and patient data, I will seek your explicit consent via a prominent in-app disclosure before any collection takes place.
