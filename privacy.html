Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Account and Professional Information

We collect the details you provide to set up and use PrescoRx on Android, including your name, email address, medical registration number, qualification, specialization, clinic details, and phone number.

Prescription and Signature Data

We store the prescription records you create. If you capture or choose a signature image, that image is saved in app storage on your Android device and used to place your signature on generated prescriptions.

Patient Data

PrescoRx allows doctors to create and store prescriptions that may include patient information such as name, age, gender, diagnosis, and prescribed medications. This data is entered by the doctor and stored securely using Firebase services. PrescoRx does not use this data for any purpose other than providing core app functionality.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to authenticate your account, complete your doctor profile, support your consultation documentation workflow, generate and manage prescriptions, restore purchases, and operate core app features. We do not sell your personal data.

Children's Privacy

PrescoRx is intended for use by licensed medical professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Google and Other Service Providers

PrescoRx uses Firebase (by Google) for authentication and secure storage of prescription and profile data. Google Sign-In may be used for account access. Google Play Billing is used for in-app purchases. AdMob is used to display non-personalized advertisements. These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share personal or patient data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Retention

Prescription and profile data are retained until you delete them or delete your account. Deleting your account removes your doctor profile, prescriptions, and locally saved signature files associated with the app.

Your Controls

From Settings you can review the Privacy Policy, sign out, or permanently delete your account and associated app data. Android permission choices can be managed at any time from Settings > Apps > PrescoRx > Permissions.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure backend infrastructure provided by Firebase. Access controls ensure that each doctor can only access their own records.

Governing Law

This Privacy Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

Consent

By using PrescoRx, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
