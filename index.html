Why Kavum?

Everything a prescribing doctor needs.

Structured prescriptions

Patient details, diagnosis, medicines with dosage and frequency - all captured in a consistent, export-ready format.

Patient history

All patients and their prescriptions in one encrypted archive. Search by name, phone, or patient ID. Pre-fill repeat visits instantly.

Telemedicine ready

Log consultation mode, record consent, and document caregiver details for minor patients - in full compliance with India's telemedicine guidelines.

End-to-end encrypted

Patient data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before leaving your device. Stored on Firebase infrastructure in Mumbai - no one else can read it.

Workflow

From consultation to shareable prescription in one pass.

  1. 1

    Set up your doctor profile

    Add your name, registration number, qualification, clinic details, and draw or upload your signature once. It appears on every prescription automatically.

  2. 2

    Capture the consultation

    Record patient info, diagnosis, and medicines in a structured form. Returning patients are pre-filled from history. Telemedicine fields are available when needed.

  3. 3

    Preview, export and share

    Review the prescription before sending. Export as a polished PDF, save as an image, or share directly to the patient's WhatsApp in one tap.
